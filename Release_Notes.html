Purpose

STM32WB-BLE-Privacy example This example implements a GAP Peripheral device with Bluetooth LE Privacy feature enabled to demonstrate advertising with a Resolvable Private Address. It includes the configuration per section 6.7 of AN5289 Rev. 6 to enable Privacy Mode.

STM32WB-BLE-Privacy example is being delivered as-is, not necessarily supported by ST.

This example includes the HAL and LL drivers provided in the STM32CubeWB_v1.13.3 package. For further information about these components, see official package.
